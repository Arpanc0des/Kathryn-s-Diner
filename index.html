Discover Our Story

Kathryn's Diner has been serving delicious meals and warm hospitality to the community for over a decade.

  • A cozy atmosphere perfect for family gatherings or a casual meal with friends.
  • Dedicated to using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients in every dish.
  • Our menu features a diverse selection of breakfast classics, sizzling grill favorites, and mouthwatering fish and chips.

At Kathryn's Diner, we believe that great food brings people together. Whether you're craving a hearty breakfast to start your day, a juicy burger fresh off the grill, or crispy fish and chips for dinner, we have something to satisfy every appetite. Join us and experience the comfort of home-cooked meals served with a smile.
